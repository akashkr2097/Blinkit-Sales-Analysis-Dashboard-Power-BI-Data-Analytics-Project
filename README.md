Blinkit Sales Dashboard – Project Explanation
1. Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Dashboard is a Power BI analytics solution built to analyze the sales performance of Blinkit grocery outlets.
The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product performance, outlet performance, and customer purchasing patterns to help business stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

The goal is to identify high-performing products, understand outlet sales distribution, and optimize inventory and marketing strategies.

2. Business Problem

Blinkit operates multiple outlets with thousands of grocery products.
The company needs to understand:

Which products generate the most revenue

Which outlet types perform best

How product categories affect sales

How location and outlet size impact revenue

What customer buying patterns look like

Without a dashboard, analyzing this data manually is time-consuming and inefficient.

3. Project Goals

The main goals of the project are:

1. Sales Performance Monitoring

Track overall sales performance across outlets and products.

2. Product Analysis

Identify the top selling item categories and products.

3. Outlet Performance Analysis

Outlet locations

4. Customer Demand Insights

Understand customer preferences such as:

Low-fat vs Regular products

5. Business Decision Support

Help management in:

Data preparation was done using Power Query:

Key steps:

Handling missing values

Standardizing item categories

Correcting fat content labels

Removing duplicate records

Creating calculated columns

Average Rating / Performance Metrics

Shows quality or customer preference indicators.

7. DAX Measures Used

Common DAX calculations include:

Total Sales
Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Item_Outlet_Sales])
Average Sales
Average Sales = AVERAGE(Sales[Item_Outlet_Sales])
Total Items
Total Items = COUNT(Sales[Item Identifier])

These measures power the dashboard KPIs.
